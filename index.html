How it works: The $10,500 Cap

Normally, the max grant is $3,500/year. However, you have unused grants from the last 10 years. By contributing more now, we trigger the "Carry Forward" provision, allowing you to claim up to the legal maximum of $10,500 per year until your backlog is empty.

🛑The Age 49 Cut-Off

The year you turn 49 is the final year the government contributes any money.

On December 31st of your 49th year, grants and bonds stop forever. After this date, you should stop contributing, as you will no longer receive the 300% match.

⚠️

The 10-Year Rule (Crucial)

To keep all the government grants and bonds (the ~$76,500 free money), you must wait 10 years after the last government deposit before making a withdrawal.

Since your last grant arrives at Age 49, you must wait until Age 59/60 to withdraw without penalty. This timing is perfect for retirement income.
